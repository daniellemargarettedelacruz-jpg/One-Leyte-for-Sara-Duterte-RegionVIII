Our Purpose

One Leyte for Sara Duterte is a voluntary movement of individuals from Leyte and across Eastern Visayas who believe in the importance of unity, civic responsibility, and freedom of choice in shaping the future of the nation.

In a time when many Filipinos are reflecting on the direction of the country, citizens have come together with a shared purpose: to help build a stronger Philippines and protect the future of the next generation.

Guided by the spirit of volunteerism and bayanihan, the movement encourages individuals from all communities to participate and contribute to the welfare of the nation.

The Story of One Leyte

One Leyte for Sara Duterte began as a gathering of individuals from Leyte and across Region VIII who share a common belief: that the people of the region should always have the freedom to express their own voice and convictions about the future of the nation.

For many years, different perceptions have existed regarding the political identity of various regions in the Philippines. However, the people of Leyte and the wider Eastern Visayas region are diverse in thought and belief. Every citizen has the right to decide, according to their conscience, the kind of leadership they believe can guide the country toward a better future.

Amid the challenges facing the nation today, many citizens have begun reflecting deeply on the direction of the country and the future that awaits the next generation. Families across communities share similar hopes and concerns for their children and grandchildren.

Out of these shared concerns and aspirations, individuals began to unite in a spirit of responsibility and civic participation. What started as conversations among concerned citizens gradually evolved into a voluntary movement built on unity, discipline, and love for the nation.

Today, One Leyte for Sara Duterte continues to grow as individuals from different municipalities, barangays, and communities come together in support of national direction that they believe can help strengthen the country and safeguard the future for generations to come.

Why Join the Movement

Many citizens today share a common concern for the future of our nation. Joining the movement is a way for individuals to stand together in unity, volunteerism, and civic responsibility for the protection of the next generation.

Unity of the People

The movement brings together citizens from different municipalities, barangays, and communities who believe that unity among the people is essential for strengthening the nation.
