Our Purpose

One Leyte for Sara Duterte is a voluntary movement of individuals from Leyte and across Eastern Visayas who believe in the importance of unity, civic responsibility, and freedom of choice in shaping the future of the nation.

Citizens have come together with a shared purpose: to help build a stronger Philippines and protect the future of the next generation.

The Story of One Leyte

One Leyte for Sara Duterte began as a grassroots gathering of individuals from Leyte and across Eastern Visayas who share a common belief: that the people of the region should always have the freedom to express their own voice and convictions about the future of the nation.

For many years, it has often been assumed that the people of Eastern Visayas move politically as one unified bloc under a single political influence. While the region shares a strong cultural identity and history, its citizens hold diverse perspectives and aspirations for the future of the country.

Members of the movement came together through voluntary participation, community dialogue, and civic engagement to demonstrate that the people of Leyte and the wider Region VIII stand for unity, responsible leadership, and the protection of future generations.

Through the spirit of volunteerism and bayanihan, the movement continues to grow as individuals from different sectors and communities unite with a shared purpose: to help strengthen the nation and safeguard the future of the next generation.

Eastern Visayas United

Eastern Visayas — composed of Leyte, Southern Leyte, Biliran, Samar, Eastern Samar, and Northern Samar — represents a region with shared heritage, resilience, and civic responsibility.

Across the region, citizens are coming together in unity and volunteerism to support responsible leadership and safeguard the future of the next generation.
