About the Movement

One Leyte for Sara Duterte is a grassroots volunteer alliance formed by individuals who believe in the values of leadership, discipline, unity, and national responsibility.

Membership in this movement is based on **free will and voluntary support**. Individuals from all sectors of society may join and participate in the advocacy to strengthen communities and help shape a better future for the next generation.

The alliance encourages unity from the **provincial level down to municipalities and barangays**, fostering cooperation and bayanihan among citizens who share the same vision.

Core Principles

  • Voluntary Participation
  • Unity and Bayanihan
  • Community Leadership
  • Service to the Nation
  • Protecting the Next Generation
