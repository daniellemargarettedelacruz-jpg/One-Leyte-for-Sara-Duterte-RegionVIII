Our Purpose

One Leyte for Sara Duterte is a voluntary movement of individuals from Leyte and across Eastern Visayas who believe in the importance of unity, civic responsibility, and freedom of choice in shaping the future of the nation.

Citizens have come together with a shared purpose: to help build a stronger Philippines and protect the future of the next generation.

The Story of One Leyte

One Leyte for Sara Duterte began as a gathering of individuals from Leyte and across Region VIII who share a common belief: that the people of the region should always have the freedom to express their own voice and convictions about the future of the nation.

Out of these shared concerns and aspirations, individuals began to unite in a spirit of responsibility and civic participation.

Eastern Visayas United

Across Leyte, Southern Leyte, Biliran, Samar, Eastern Samar, and Northern Samar, citizens are coming together in unity and volunteerism to support responsible leadership and safeguard the future of the next generation.
